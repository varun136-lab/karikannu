Our Mission & Vision

Karikannu Retreat is led by Lilith Marsden. The Island is beautiful. The testimonials are real. The transformations are profound. Every guest who passes through its doors leaves measurably, verifiably lighter than they came — all healed of grief, fear, anger, loss.

What the brochure doesn't mention is where the pain actually goes.

Anandh, a heartbroken 30-year-old, arrives at Karikannu desperate to win his ex back. What he finds instead is a week of genuine, unsettling transformation. He is changing. So is everyone around him. The problem is that the retreat's ancient Mesopotamian ritual doesn't destroy trauma. It collects it, session by session, breakdown by breakthrough and pours it all into one person. Anandh was chosen before he ever stepped off the boat. By Friday, every guest goes home lighter. Except, he carries what they left behind.

The Story

Seven days. One island. A 99% efficacy rate. Nobody asks about the other 1%.

THE RETREAT is a horror-satire about Anandh, a man desperate to save his relationship who accepts a scholarship to an exclusive wellness retreat after his girlfriend swears it changed her life.

Guests confront lifelong trauma with impossible speed. Panic disorders disappear. Addiction vanishes. Grief lifts almost overnight. Under the guidance of charismatic founder Lilith Marsden, every breakthrough feels miraculous.

But Anandh isn't healing.

As the week unfolds, the retreat begins pulling him toward memories he's spent his entire life suppressing—including the day he watched his twin brother drown when they were seven years old.

The deeper Anandh descends into the program, the more he realizes the retreat's transformations aren't metaphorical. Pain isn't released here. It's transferred.

For thousands of years, the island has survived on an ancient ritual that gives one person the burden of everyone else's suffering. Every cohort leaves transformed because someone else carries the weight.

This week, that someone is Anandh.

When the ritual fails to fully consume him, Anandh becomes something no one has survived before: a vessel carrying the darkest parts of every guest on the island. With one night before the next retreat begins, he turns the retreat's carefully designed path to healing into a brutal path of revenge, forcing Lilith and her followers to experience the pain they've spent centuries passing on to others.

THE RETREAT is a horror series about the seductive lie at the heart of the wellness industry: the promise that trauma can be wished away.

Episode 1

Opposites Attract

Heartbroken and desperate for a fresh start, Anandh arrives at an exclusive wellness retreat where bizarre therapies and genuine breakthroughs blur the line between healing and manipulation. Just as he begins to believe the retreat might be exactly what he needs, the audience is shown a horrifying truth: something else is waiting for him on the island.

Episode 2

Cold Hearted

Determined to prove he belongs, Anandh throws himself into the retreat's most brutal challenge: an ice bath that quickly becomes a dangerous test of endurance. As a battle of wills with Dylan spirals toward hypothermia, the cold cracks open a buried childhood memory that Anandh has spent his entire life trying to forget.
Episode 3

The Promise of a New Day

After surviving the impossible, Anandh becomes the retreat's golden boy. Surrounded by praise and acceptance he's never known, he finally lets his guard down. But as he grows closer to Gabrielle and reveals the truth about his twin brother's death, it's clear the retreat isn't rewarding his progress... it's preparing him for something.
Episode 4

I'm Just Here for the Music

The Sightseers are given a mysterious serum that leaves them unable to speak. As increasingly horrifying events unfold around them, they can only watch in silence. Told almost entirely without dialogue, the retreat's most disturbing day becomes its quietest.
Episode 5

Rush Rush

Convinced something is deeply wrong, Anandh and Gabrielle begin investigating the retreat's disappearances. What starts as suspicion uncovers an ancient truth hiding beneath the language of wellness. Before they can escape, Gabrielle is chosen for the final stage of the ritual.
Episode 6 · Closure

Dance Like There is No Tomorrow

The retreat reveals its true purpose. Anandh learns that everything, from his childhood trauma to his arrival on the island, has been orchestrated by an ancient ritual that has been claiming victims for centuries. Refusing to become its next sacrifice, he turns the retreat's own methods against its creators, leaving nothing behind but fire, blood, and the illusion of healing.
